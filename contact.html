Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How long does support take to respond?
We typically respond within minutes during business hours (8 PM - 11 PM PST weekdays, 3 PM - 1 AM PST weekends). Even outside these hours, we strive to reply within a few hours at most.
What information should I include when contacting support?
Please include your username, email address, a detailed description of the issue, any error messages you're seeing, and steps to reproduce the problem if possible.
Do you offer refunds for Golden credits?
Yes! We offer refunds at any time for any amount of Golden in your balance. No ID is required - just provide your crypto address and we'll send your refund.
Is my data secure with GoldenSpaceAI?
Absolutely. We use top-tier SSL security to ensure your information, Golden balance, and email remain completely private and inaccessible to anyone but you.
The AI isn't producing quality results. What can I do?
Try being more specific in your prompts, providing examples of what you want, or breaking complex tasks into smaller steps. If problems persist, contact us for personalized assistance.
