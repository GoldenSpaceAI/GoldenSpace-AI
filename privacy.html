Privacy Policy

Last updated:

1. Information We Collect

When you use GoldenSpaceAI, we may collect basic information such as your name, email address, and plan details when you sign in with Google. We also record usage counts (like how many questions you’ve asked today) to enforce fair plan limits.

We do not store images or voice recordings from features like “Speak” or homework uploads. Any media is processed in real-time and not saved to our servers.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve GoldenSpaceAI’s features.
  • To enforce plan limits and prevent abuse.
  • To process payments via our partner Paddle.
  • To communicate important account or service updates.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing. Data is only shared with service providers necessary to run GoldenSpaceAI (e.g., Paddle for payments, Google for authentication).

4. Data Security

We use secure servers and HTTPS encryption. While no online system is 100% secure, we take industry-standard steps to protect your data.

5. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and personal data at any time by contacting support@goldenspaceai.space.

6. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be posted here with a new “Last updated” date.
